About Us

The Windjammer Marinette is a full-service casual dining establishment offering local favorites and updated classics. We provide a cozy dining experience for the whole family, with a bar that caters to customers looking for specialty cocktails, wine, and local beers. Our nautical decor adds to the fun environment, making it a great place to stop for lunch or dinner.
